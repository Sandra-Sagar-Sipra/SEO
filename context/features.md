## Common Objections & Answers

### "We've tried AI before and it didn't work."
**Answer**: 85% of AI projects fail — almost always because the wrong problem was chosen, not because of bad technology. Our Double Diamond process spends two weeks on problem definition before writing a single line of code. We model ROI for every use case before we commit.

### "We're not big enough for enterprise AI."
**Answer**: Our Packaged Solutions start at $197/month. Our Discovery Sprints are 2–4 weeks. We serve companies from 15 employees to enterprise scale — and mid-market is our sweet spot.

### "We don't have enough data."
**Answer**: Data readiness is one of the first things we assess. Many companies have more usable data than they think — and we've delivered production systems with surprisingly lean datasets. Our AI Readiness Assessment tells you exactly where you stand.

### "How do we know the ROI is real?"
**Answer**: We model ROI before we build, track it during delivery, and measure it after launch. Our 3.2x average ROI figure comes from 40+ production systems with agreed KPIs, not projections.

### "Will this create vendor dependency?"
**Answer**: No. We work in your cloud environment, in your codebase, with your team. Everything we build, you own. Knowledge transfer is part of every engagement so your team can maintain and extend the system after we hand it over.
