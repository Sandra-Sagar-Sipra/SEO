## Internal Linking Best Practices for Aiquire Content

1. **Lead with the proof**: Link to case studies when making any performance claim
2. **Service page over homepage**: Link to specific service pages (e.g., `/services/ai-ml-development`) rather than the homepage
3. **CTA at natural close points**: Every article should include a discovery call or audit link — placed after establishing the problem or at article end
4. **3–5 links per post**: Aim for 3–5 internal links; don't cluster them all at the end
5. **Descriptive anchor text**: "our MLOps service" beats "click here"; "how we reduced resolution time by 30%" beats "case study"
6. **Update this map**: Add new blog posts and case studies as they're published
